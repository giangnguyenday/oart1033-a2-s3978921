The Dawn of Newness
This 3D typographical sculpture is included in SOL61 - a first group project in my first year at university. Since the project's aim was about spreading positive message of leaving comfort zone to grow and become a better version, I wanted to convey the common characteristic of both things and how people might feel when they experience aspects that are no longer in their safe zone.
